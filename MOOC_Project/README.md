Instructions for 21CSP303T-MOOC Course
	Select MOOC courses strictly from the enclosed approved list of subjects.
	Choose courses that complement your existing knowledge base and enhance your skill set.
	Each course has a duration of 12 weeks and is aligned with the academic syllabus.
	The course carries a weightage of 3 credits.
	Faculty will monitor student attendance and progress weekly, based on assessment completion. (Weekly)
	Marks obtained upon successful course completion will be recorded directly in the academia after certification submission.
	Students failing the course must complete it in the subsequent semester to earn credits.
	Do not select a course that has already been completed as a Professional or Open Elective in the 4th, 5th, or 6th semester.
	Engage consistently with course content and complete all assessments and assignments within the deadlines.
	Seek faculty guidance in case of any doubts or concerns regarding course suitability.
	Non-compliance with these guidelines may necessitate retaking the course to earn the required credits.
	Active participation and adherence to all rules are mandatory for successful credit acquisition.
